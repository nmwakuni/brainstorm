## üí≥ Transaction Fees (Primary Revenue)

### Standard Model
**Who pays**: Employee
**When**: Per advance transaction
**Rate**: 4% of advance amount

**Example**:
```
Employee advances KES 5,000
Fee: KES 200 (4%)
Employee receives: KES 4,800 in M-Pesa
(or pays back KES 5,200 at month-end)
```

### Why Employees Will Pay

**Alternative costs**:
- Loan shark: KES 1,000 (20% for 2 weeks) üí∏
- Bank overdraft: KES 500 (10% + fees) üí∏
- Shylock: KES 1,500 (30% for month) üí∏
- **Our service: KES 200 (4%)** ‚úÖ

**Value props**:
- Instant (60 seconds vs 2-3 days)
- No credit check
- No paperwork
- Works 24/7
- Direct to M-Pesa

### Volume Discounts (Loyalty Program)

| Tier | Criteria | Fee |
|------|----------|-----|
| New User | First advance | 4% |
| Regular | 2-5 advances total | 3.5% |
| Loyal | 6+ advances total | 3% |
