## üöÄ Beta Program Structure

### Phase 1: Onboarding (Week 1)

#### For Each Company:

**Day 1: Kickoff Call** (30 min)
- Explain how it works
- Set expectations
- Collect employee roster
- Configure advance rules

**Day 2-3: Setup**
- Upload employees to platform
- Generate invite codes
- Test M-Pesa integration
- Create training materials

**Day 4: Employee Training** (30-45 min)
- Schedule session at their office (or Zoom)
- Walk through app download
- Demo advance request
- Answer questions
- Get first advances processed

**Day 5-7: Early Support**
- Monitor usage closely
- Respond to issues within 15 min
- Fix any bugs immediately
- Daily check-in with HR contact

---

### Phase 2: Active Beta (Week 2-12)

#### Weekly Routine:

**Monday**: Email check-in
```

- What can we improve?

Usage last week:
- X employees registered
```

**Wednesday**: Monitor metrics
- Track active users
- Track advance volume
- Check M-Pesa success rate
- Identify drop-offs

**Friday**: Product updates
- Ship bug fixes
- Release new features
- Send changelog

#### Monthly Business Review:

**30-minute call**:
1. Review usage stats
2. Collect qualitative feedback
3. Discuss ROI observed
4. Plan for next month

**Metrics to review**:
- Employee adoption rate
- Advances per employee
- M-Pesa success rate
- Support ticket volume
- Employee satisfaction (survey)

## üêõ Bug Reporting Process

### For Beta Customers

**How to Report**:
1. WhatsApp: [Your number]
2. Email: beta@salaryadvance.co.ke
3. In-app: "Report Issue" button

**What to Include**:
- What happened?
- What were you trying to do?
- Screenshot (if possible)
- Time it happened
- Affected user (employee ID or phone)

**Response SLA**:
- Critical (M-Pesa failed): 15 min
- High (Can't request advance): 1 hour
- Medium (UI issue): 4 hours
- Low (Feature request): 24 hours

### Bug Tracking

**Priorities**:
- P0: Showstopper (M-Pesa broken)
- P1: Major (Can't request advance)
- P2: Minor (UI glitch)
- P3: Nice to have (feature request)

**Fix Timeline**:
- P0: Same day
- P1: Within 48 hours
- P2: Next sprint (1 week)
- P3: Backlog
